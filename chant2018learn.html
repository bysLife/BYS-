FAQ


What happens if I don't finish my rounds for the day?

If you don't complete your rounds for the day, you can complete them the next day and then log them.



Where can I get my own Japa beads?

I have a few extra available. If you already have your own, make sure they are made of Tulasi wood and have 108 beads total.



What do I focus on during the meditation?

Proper pronunciation and hearing of the mantra. The power is in the sound vibration!



Where do I log my rounds?

Click here for the link to the spreadsheet



When's the best time to chant?

The most beneficial time to chant is first thing in the morning when you first wake up. If you look at meditation in the form of credits in a bank account - you receive more benefit or more credits by practicing earlier in the morning. The credit you receive back decreases throughout the day



How can I stay consistent?

The best way is to have a set place and time you dedicate to completing your meditation. When you have something set like this, there is less chance you'll miss your practice!
